Card 1: What is AI?

AI (Artificial Intelligence) is like a smart helper that learns from examples. In Andhra Pradesh, think of AI as a weather predictor that helps farmers decide when to plant crops.

Learning from Data

Story: AI looks at lots of past weather data and learns patterns. "When clouds gather, rain comes."

Fun Fact: Andhra Pradesh farmers use AI apps to predict monsoon rains.

Making Predictions

Story: Based on learning, AI predicts: "Tomorrow might rain – plant seeds today!"

Fun Fact: AI helps detect pests on mango trees before they spread.

Helping People

Story: AI isn't magic; it's a tool made by humans to solve problems, like better farming or healthcare.

Fun Fact: In Telugu villages, AI chatbots answer questions about government schemes.

Key Technical Terms

  • Artificial Intelligence (AI): Machines that think and learn like humans.
  • Machine Learning: AI that improves by learning from data.
  • Data: Information used to train AI, like weather records.
  • Prediction: AI guessing what might happen next based on patterns.
  • Algorithm: The rules AI follows to learn and decide.

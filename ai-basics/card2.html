Card 2: Machine Learning Basics

Machine Learning (ML) is how AI learns. Like a child learning to recognize mangoes by seeing many examples.

Learning from Examples

Story: Show AI photos of healthy and sick mango leaves. It learns patterns.

Fun Fact: Andhra Pradesh farmers use ML to spot pests early.

Making Predictions

Story: After learning, AI predicts if a new leaf is sick or healthy.

Fun Fact: ML predicts crop yields based on weather data.

Improving Over Time

Story: More examples make AI better. Like practicing Telugu to speak fluently.

Fun Fact: AI gets smarter with more data from Andhra Pradesh farms.

Key Technical Terms

  • Machine Learning: AI that learns from data without being programmed for every case.
  • Training Data: Examples used to teach the AI.
  • Model: The AI's "brain" after learning.
  • Prediction: What the AI guesses based on learning.
  • Algorithm: The math rules for learning.

Card 2: Building a Resume

A resume is your story on paper. It tells employers about your skills and experiences. Like a biodata for marriage, but for jobs.

Personal Information

Story: Name, phone, email, address. Keep it simple and professional.

Fun Fact: In Andhra Pradesh, include your village and district.

Education and Skills

Story: List schools, any computer courses. Add skills like "Basic Computer Use".

Fun Fact: Mention online learning from Coursera or YouTube.

Work Experience

Story: Any jobs, even small ones. Describe what you did.

Fun Fact: If no jobs, list volunteer work or family help.

Key Technical Terms

  • Resume: A one-page summary of your background.
  • Objective: A short statement of your job goals.
  • Skills Section: List abilities like typing or software knowledge.
  • References: People who can speak about your work (ask permission).
  • ATS-Friendly: Format that computers can read for job sites.

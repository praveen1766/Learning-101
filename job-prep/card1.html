Card 1: Getting Started with IT Jobs

Landing an IT job is like preparing for a festival. In Andhra Pradesh, think of it as getting ready for Ugadi – gather skills, build confidence, and apply!

Build Your Resume

Story: List your skills and experiences. Include computer basics, any online courses.

Fun Fact: Andhra Pradesh IT companies look for local talent with basic skills.

Find Job Openings

Story: Search on sites like Naukri or LinkedIn for "IT jobs in Hyderabad".

Fun Fact: Many startups in Andhra Pradesh hire for data entry or support roles.

Prepare for Interviews

Story: Practice talking about your skills. "I learned basic computers and want to grow."

Fun Fact: Be confident – many jobs start with training!

Key Technical Terms

  • Resume: A document listing your skills, experience, and education.
  • Job Application: The process of applying for a job opening.
  • Interview: A meeting where employers ask questions to learn about you.
  • Skills: Abilities you have, like using a computer or basic programming.
  • Portfolio: A collection of your work or projects to show employers.

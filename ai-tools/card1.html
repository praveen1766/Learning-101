Card 1: Introduction to AI Tools

AI tools are like smart apps that help you do tasks faster. In Andhra Pradesh, think of ChatGPT as a helpful assistant that answers questions about Telugu culture or farming tips.

Chatbots Like ChatGPT

Story: Ask questions and get answers. "How to grow better tomatoes?" AI responds with advice.

Fun Fact: Andhra Pradesh students use AI to learn English-Telugu translations.

Data Analysis Tools

Story: AI analyzes numbers. Farmers input crop data, AI suggests best selling prices.

Fun Fact: Small businesses in Vijayawada use free AI tools for sales reports.

Image Recognition

Story: Show a photo of a plant, AI says if it's healthy or has pests.

Fun Fact: Andhra Pradesh apps use AI to identify local fruits and vegetables.

Key Technical Terms

  • AI Tools: Software that uses AI to perform tasks.
  • Chatbot: An AI program that talks like a human, answering questions.
  • Data Analysis: Using AI to find patterns in numbers or information.
  • Image Recognition: AI that identifies objects or people in photos.
  • API: A way for apps to connect and use AI services.

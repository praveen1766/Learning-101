Card 3: Creating Images with AI

Did you know AI can create images from just text? It's like describing a scene to an artist who then paints it for you. This is called AI Image Generation and it's a fun and powerful tool.

1. Writing a Prompt

Story: You start by writing a description of the image you want. This is called a "prompt." The more detailed your prompt, the better the result.

Example Prompt: "A colorful painting of a peacock dancing in a mango grove in Andhra Pradesh."

2. The AI Creates the Image

Story: The AI tool takes your prompt and generates a completely new image based on your description. It understands objects, colors, and styles from your words.

Fun Fact: Some AI tools can create images in the style of famous artists!

3. Popular AI Image Tools

Story: There are many tools you can try for free to create your own AI images. Some popular ones are Midjourney, DALL-E 2, and Stable Diffusion.

Use Case: You can use these to create a logo for a new shop in your village or just make beautiful art for fun.

Key Technical Terms

  • Image Generation: The process of creating images from text descriptions using AI.
  • Prompt: The text you write to describe the image you want the AI to create.
  • Generative AI: A type of AI that can create new content, like text, images, or music.
  • DALL-E 2 & Midjourney: Popular and powerful AI tools for generating images from prompts.
  • Diffusion Model: The technology behind many modern AI image generators.

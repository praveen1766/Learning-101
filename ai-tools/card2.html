Card 2: Using ChatGPT for Learning

ChatGPT is a helpful AI chatbot. Ask it questions and get answers. Like having a tutor who speaks Telugu and English.

Asking Questions

Story: Type: "Explain computers in simple Telugu." ChatGPT responds helpfully.

Fun Fact: Andhra Pradesh students use it for homework help.

Getting Ideas

Story: Ask: "Ideas for small business in Vijayawada." It gives suggestions.

Fun Fact: Farmers ask about better irrigation methods.

Learning Languages

Story: Practice English: "Translate 'Hello' to Telugu." It helps learn.

Fun Fact: Use it to improve communication skills for jobs.

Key Technical Terms

  • ChatGPT: An AI chatbot that answers questions and helps with tasks.
  • Prompt: The question or instruction you give to the AI.
  • Response: The answer the AI gives back.
  • Conversational AI: AI designed to chat like a human.
  • Free Tier: Basic use without paying, available to everyone.
